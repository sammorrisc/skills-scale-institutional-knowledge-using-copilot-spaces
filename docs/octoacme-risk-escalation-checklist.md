# OctoAcme Risk Escalation Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure all critical project risks are identified, monitored, and escalated/mitigated quickly:

- [ ] Risk logged in register with owner and impact level
- [ ] Risk reviewed in weekly status meeting
- [ ] Change Manager and relevant stakeholders notified for high/critical risks
- [ ] Escalation plan documented (actions, deadlines, responsible roles)
- [ ] Communication sent to impacted team members and leaders
- [ ] Follow-up action items assigned and tracked
- [ ] Resolution or mitigation tracked in decision log
